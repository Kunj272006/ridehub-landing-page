## ✨ Features

RideHub is built with a focus on user experience and efficiency. Our key features include:

*   **HubX Integration:** Our proprietary system, HubX, is the core of RideHub. It intelligently manages vehicle allocation, identity verification, and ride tracking to ensure a seamless rental process.
*   **Clean UI:** A modern, intuitive, and user-friendly interface designed for ease of navigation and quick access to services.
*   **Student-Centric:** Tailored specifically to the needs and preferences of the student community.

## 📍 How to Use

Embark on your RideHub journey with these simple steps:

### Process
1.  📱 **Sign Up:** Create your account in under 60 seconds. HubX verifies your identity instantly, ensuring a secure and swift onboarding experience.
2.  ✦ **HubX Matches You:** HubX intelligently assigns a verified SpeedWheelz vehicle located nearest to your current position, minimizing wait times.
3.  ⏳ **Set Your Hours:** Select your rental duration, ranging from 1 hour to 7 days. The fare is calculated instantly, providing complete transparency with no hidden charges.
4.  🧾 **Ride & Invoice:** HubX tracks your ride in real-time, processes your payment securely, and automatically delivers your invoice upon completion.

## 💡 Installation

As a web application, RideHub does not require traditional installation. You can access it directly through your web browser. For development purposes, please refer to the GitHub repository for setup instructions.
